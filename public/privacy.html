AI Jobs Doomsday Clock

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-06

AI Jobs Doomsday Clock is an independent public research project available at jobdoomsday.tech and through related app experiences. This policy explains what information is collected and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • We do not require account creation to browse the public site.
  • We may receive basic technical data that is standard for website hosting, such as IP address, browser type, and request logs.
  • The app may store local settings on your device, such as subscription state needed for StoreKit testing and saved watchlist items.

How information is used

  • To deliver the site and app content.
  • To maintain service reliability and investigate technical issues.
  • To support subscription features and restore purchases through Apple's systems.

Third-party services

Purchases and subscription management are handled through Apple. External links in the project may take you to third-party websites such as GitHub, BLS, or source articles, which operate under their own policies.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only with service providers required to host the site, deliver app functionality, or process subscriptions.

Data retention

Local app data remains on your device unless you remove the app or clear it through normal platform controls. Server-side logs are retained only as needed for operations and security.

Contact

For questions about this policy, use the support channel at GitHub Issues.
